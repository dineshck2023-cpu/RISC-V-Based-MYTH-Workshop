Makerchip Introduction:
Makerchip is an online platform used for writing,
simulating, and testing Verilog and TL-Verilog
designs directly in a web browser. It helps students,
beginners, and engineers learn digital circuit design
without installing complex software. Makerchip
provides features such as code editing, waveform
viewing, and diagram visualization in one
environment, making hardware design and
verification easier.
